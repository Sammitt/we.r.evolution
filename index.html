Nós somos Evolução

(r)Evolution is a cool (we hope!) team from Brazil who is slowly spreading its wings.
The team was formed in 2012, but was re-birthed in 2015 with a focus on growing the Ultimate community in Brazil and developing a solid culture of practice and growth, always pushing to be an example in inclusiveness, development and spirit.

We have a very diverse group of players from a few satellite cities around São Paulo, as well as a few different nationalities - Ucranian, Colombian, American, French - who live in here.
We love to meet new people, engage in games, challenge each other and grow our team and sport. Windmill has been a dream tournament we've been considering for the past year.
